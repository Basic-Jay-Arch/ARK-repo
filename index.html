ARK Ecosolutions

Mission Statement:

ARK Ecosolutions, LLC is a premier ecological landscaping and environmental solutions company that blends native habitat with ecological forward property design to create a synergistic landscape philosophy. We strive to provide the best combination of science, creativity, and sustainability to create the low maintenance and eco-friendly habitat solutions our clients are seeking to resolve.

What We Do

Landscape design:

  • How to best utilize certain aspects of your property and develop it to be your ideal property

  • We focus on the most efficient ways that your property can be cheaper, lower maintenance and sustainable long-term

  • Choosing plants that are native and that are best suited for the purposes you are seeking for your property

  • Discuss what you (or should it be client?) envision for your property, and we design a plan to meet your vision.

Riparian Buffer and Stream Bank Restoration:

  • Designing affordable solutions to help abate and prevent your property and soil from eroding into an adjacent water body

  • We have simple and affordable solutions to keep your property size the same, and help prevent your soil from eroding into the stream.

Invasive Species Reduction:

  • We focus on the most efficient way to replace or at least reduce the invasives on your property for native ones that are better adapted for your property type and the local ecosystem

Lawn to Meadow Conversions and Habitat Restoration:

  • Creates a beautiful hybrid of manicured spaces with a naturalized design that increases property value, helps mitigate flooding, decreases erosion on your property.

  • Reduces your carbon footprint, more sustainable, cheaper to maintain (mow only a few times a year), decreases stormwater runoff, and the pollinators love it!
